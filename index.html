About Me

I call Utah home - I was born in Ogden, Utah, in 1979 and have spent most of my life here in The Beehive State. I've spent three years living on two other continents, speak two languages, and have been immersed in the cultures of South America and the Pashtun tribes of the Aghan/Packistani border region known as Bachlocistan.

I am married to a wonderful woman who also happens to be an amazing mother and a fantastic nurse. Her name is Becca, not "Rebecca" or "Becky". Becca and I met at Utah State University in Logan, Utah, in the beginning of 2002. We had a fast introduction, courtship, and were married in June of that same year.

We have three children who provide the primary purpose of our lives and marriage. We love raising our kids here where we grew up in the same value system that shaped our lives and goals.
